## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds sweet potatoes, unpeeled, cut lengthwise into 1-inch wedges
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
- 1 tablespoon table salt, divided
- 2 1/2 teaspoons pepper, divided
- 6 scallions, trimmed
- 1 tablespoon packed dark brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon finely ground coffee
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 2 (1-pound) boneless strip steaks, 1 to 1 1/2 inches thick
- 2 radishes, trimmed and sliced thin
- 1 tablespoon lime juice, plus lime wedges for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 450°F.
2. Toss sweet potatoes with 1 1/2 tablespoons oil, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1 teaspoon pepper. Arrange on baking sheet.
3. Roast about 25 minutes.
4. Toss scallions with remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper.
5. Mix brown sugar, coffee, chili powder, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, and remaining 1 teaspoon pepper. Pat steaks dry. Rub with mixture.
6. Place scallions on potatoes. Place steaks on empty half of sheet.
7. Roast until steaks register 125°F (medium-rare), 12 to 15 minutes.
9. Toss radishes, lime juice, and remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt.
10. Serve it!

## Notes

- This is a sheet pan recipe, not cast iron — "one-pan" refers to the rimmed baking sheet.
- The coffee rub adds browning and flavor without tasting like coffee.
- Use the rest time — carryover will bring steaks to ~130°F for medium-rare.

## Ingredients

- 1 loaf Italian or French bread
- 1 stick (½ cup) butter, softened
- 4 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Mix butter, garlic, parsley, Parmesan, and salt.
3. Split bread lengthwise. Spread butter mixture generously on both halves.
4. Place on a sheet pan, cut-side up.
5. Bake 10–12 minutes until edges are golden and butter is bubbling.
6. Slice and serve immediately.

## Notes

- For extra crisp, finish under the broiler for 60 seconds. Watch it closely — broilers don't forgive.
- Make the compound butter in bulk and freeze it in a log. Slice off rounds whenever you need garlic bread.
